## Get In Touch

We welcome collaboration, feedback, and inquiries from the security research community. Whether you're interested in contributing research, have questions about our work, or want to discuss potential collaborations, we'd love to hear from you.

### Contact Methods

**Email**: [contact@talkingrrocklabs.com](mailto:contact@talkingrrocklabs.com) *(placeholder)*

**GitHub**: [github.com/talking-rock-labs](https://github.com/talking-rock-labs) *(placeholder)*

**Twitter**: [@TalkingRockLabs](https://twitter.com/TalkingRockLabs) *(placeholder)*

### Collaboration Opportunities

We're always interested in collaborating with security researchers on:

* Joint research projects
* Tool development initiatives
* Guest blog posts and writeups
* Conference presentations
* Security challenges and CTFs

### Responsible Disclosure

If you've discovered a security vulnerability in any of our published tools or projects, please contact us privately at:

**Security Email**: [security@talkingrrocklabs.com](mailto:security@talkingrrocklabs.com) *(placeholder)*

We follow responsible disclosure practices and will work with you to address any security concerns.

### Response Time

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 48-72 hours. For security-related matters, we aim to provide an initial response within 24 hours.

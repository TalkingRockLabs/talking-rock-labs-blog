### Crebain: Covert C2 Over LoRa Mesh Networks

**Date:** May 2025
**Location:** BSides Tampa 2025
**Researchers:** Mike Seay, Michael Music

An introduction to Crebain, a command and control framework designed for covert operations using LoRa radio and the Reticulum Network Stack. This talk covers the motivation behind out-of-band C2 communications, the architecture of Crebain, and live demonstrations of the tool in action.

### Crebain: Covert C2 Over LoRa Mesh Networks

**Date:** May 2025
**Location:** BSides Nashville 2025
**Researchers:** Mike Seay, Michael Music

Initial proof-of-concept presentation introducing the concept of using LoRa mesh networking for covert command and control operations. Discussed the limitations of traditional C2 egress methods and how RF-based communications can bypass network security controls entirely.
